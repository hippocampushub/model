Digital Reconstructions

Neurons

We reconstructed the 3D morphology of single neurons and classified them into morphological types (m-types). In addition, we recorded electrical traces from the same cell types and classified the traces into electrical types (e-types). Finally, we mapped the e-types expressed in each m-type to account for the observed diversity of morpho-electrical subtypes (me-types).
